Tanjiro Kamado (Japanese: 竈門 炭治郎, Hepburn: Kamado Tanjirō) is a fictional character and

the main protagonist in Koyoharu Gotouge's manga Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. Tanjiro

goes on a quest to restore the humanity of his sister, Nezuko, who was turned into a demon after

his family was killed by Muzan Kibutsuji following an attack that resulted in the death of his other

relatives. After an encounter with Giyu Tomioka demon slayer, Tanjiro is recruited by Giyu to also

become a demon slayer to help his sister turn human again and avenge his family.
